;; Ordering Preferences
;; Put all the NearStart/NearEnd rules here to keep track of them.
;; The items are listed in priority order, the first has highest priority.
;; So "Mashed Lists.esp" should always be the first in the NearEnd
;; rule in order to ensure it loads very near the end of the load order.
;;
;; Note: overuse of NearStart/NearEnd will render them meaningless.
;; They are intended to be use only for very special circumstances.

;; order. I'm actually loathe to add NearStart or NearEnd rules for mods,

;; should try to be impartial. On the other hand, if a user has loaded
;; NOM, they obviously expect it to work, and the best way to do that
